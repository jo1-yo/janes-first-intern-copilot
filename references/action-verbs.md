# Action Verbs

Every bullet starts with an active verb — it's the A in C-A-R. Pick the verb that says most precisely what the user did; vary verbs so no verb opens more than two bullets on the page.

## Verbs to kill on sight

"Responsible for", "Helped with", "Assisted in", "Participated in", "Worked on", "Was involved in", "Utilized" (say *used*, or better, name the action the tool served).

Also ration the AI-flavored power verbs — **spearheaded, leveraged, orchestrated, championed** — screeners now read them as machine-written. Plain and precise beats inflated: *built, led, wrote, launched, won*.

## By category

**Leadership & management:** led, directed, ran, coordinated, chaired, founded, organized, oversaw, supervised, delegated, recruited, trained, mentored, mobilized

**Building & engineering:** built, developed, engineered, implemented, coded, shipped, launched, deployed, automated, integrated, debugged, migrated, prototyped, architected

**Research & analysis:** researched, analyzed, investigated, measured, evaluated, tested, modeled, surveyed, benchmarked, identified, validated, synthesized, quantified

**Communication & writing:** wrote, authored, presented, pitched, documented, edited, translated, published, negotiated, interviewed, taught, briefed

**Creative & design:** designed, illustrated, produced, filmed, composed, storyboarded, prototyped, rebranded, animated, curated

**Marketing & growth:** grew, marketed, promoted, launched, positioned, tested (A/B), converted, expanded, monetized, partnered

**Operations & organization:** streamlined, scheduled, budgeted, tracked, standardized, reduced, consolidated, maintained, processed, resolved

**Achievement & results:** won, earned, raised, increased, decreased, improved, delivered, exceeded, achieved, secured, saved

**Initiative:** initiated, created, established, introduced, proposed, pioneered, redesigned, revamped, transformed

## Tense rule

Current roles in present tense, finished roles in past tense — consistently. A page that mixes "develops" and "developed" for the same job fails the mechanical-error check.
